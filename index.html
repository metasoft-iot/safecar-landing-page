Terms and Conditions of Service

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SafeCar, a service provided by MetaSoft. By using our IoT device, mobile application, or web dashboard, you agree to these terms tailored to ensure transparency and ethical use of technology.

2. Use of IoT Data

SafeCar collects real-time telemetry from your vehicle's ECU (Engine Control Unit) solely for diagnostic and preventive maintenance purposes. We do not track your location history for advertising purposes.

3. User Responsibilities

You are responsible for the proper installation of the OBD-II device. SafeCar provides diagnostic suggestions based on data, but final repair decisions must be validated by a certified professional.

4. Privacy & Ethics

In accordance with the ACM/IEEE Software Engineering Code of Ethics, we commit to protecting your personal data. Your vehicle's health data is encrypted and will only be shared with workshops you explicitly authorize.

5. Liability

SafeCar is an assistive tool. MetaSoft is not liable for mechanical failures not detected by the system or for accidents resulting from the misuse of the application while driving.
