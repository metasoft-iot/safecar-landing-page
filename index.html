About the App

SafeCar uses advanced IoT technology to connect your vehicle to an intelligent sensor network. These sensors collect real-time data on engine performance, component status, and other key parameters. The information is processed using artificial intelligence algorithms to provide you with accurate diagnostics, personalized alerts, and proactive maintenance recommendations.
